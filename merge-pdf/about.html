Our Story

Why We Built PDFBoon

A short timeline of how PDFBoon came to be.

The Problem

Too Many Clunky PDF Tools

Most online PDF tools were slow, cluttered with ads, or required uploading sensitive files to unknown servers. We wanted something better.

The Idea

Everything, Right in the Browser

We set out to build PDF tools that run entirely client-side — no uploads, no waiting, no compromise on privacy or speed.

The Build

PDFBoon Was Born

Starting with Compress and Image to PDF, we expanded into Merge, Split, and Lock — each designed with the same simple, clean philosophy.

Today

Growing, One Tool at a Time

PDFBoon keeps growing with new tools like PDF to Word and Unlock PDF on the way — always free, always private.
